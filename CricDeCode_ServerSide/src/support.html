Why is my Debit/Credit card not accepted when I am trying to purchase?

For Indian Users,

As per the new mandate issued by the Reserve Bank of India(RBI) concerning online International transactions, card owners will need to take special permit from the bank for International transactions. This mandate is in effect since 30th June, 2013. Please check with your bank if your card is enabled for International transactions. You can read the official notice here.
