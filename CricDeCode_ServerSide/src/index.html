What does CricDeCode do?

CricDeCode brings professional level analysis available to International cricketers to the hands of every cricket player. If you are playing cricket for your school/college or playing at the highest level, the app will help you decide your goals and track your progress. Enter a few numbers into the app and get the analysis of your performance, spot problem areas and continuously improve your game. Since its launch, CricDeCode has been creating a buzz among cricketers.

Features:

Intuitive interface to enter the details of your performance
Maintain reviews for every match
Professional level performance analysis
Intuitive Graphs for personal profiling
Filter the matches to view past performances
Various filtering options to analyse performances as required
Strategizing the game plan on the go
Share success with friends, family and fans through social media
Cloud backup to store career data safely
